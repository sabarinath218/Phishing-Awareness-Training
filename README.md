# Phishing Awareness Training

## Overview

Phishing Awareness Training is a cybersecurity education project designed to help individuals recognize, prevent, and respond to phishing attacks. The training covers common phishing techniques, social engineering tactics, fake websites, and suspicious emails through engaging content, real-world examples, and interactive quizzes.

## Objectives

- Understand what phishing is and how it works.
- Learn to identify phishing emails and fraudulent websites.
- Recognize social engineering techniques used by attackers.
- Apply cybersecurity best practices to stay protected.
- Improve awareness through examples and interactive assessments.

## Topics Covered

### 1. Introduction to Phishing
- Definition of phishing
- Why phishing attacks are successful
- Impact on individuals and organizations

### 2. Types of Phishing Attacks
- Email Phishing
- Spear Phishing
- Smishing (SMS Phishing)
- Vishing (Voice Phishing)
- Clone Phishing

### 3. Recognizing Phishing Emails
- Suspicious sender addresses
- Urgent or threatening language
- Unexpected attachments
- Fake login requests
- Malicious links

### 4. Identifying Fake Websites
- Verifying domain names
- Checking HTTPS certificates
- Detecting look-alike URLs
- Website legitimacy checks

### 5. Social Engineering Tactics
- Authority
- Urgency
- Fear
- Curiosity
- Trust exploitation

### 6. Real-World Examples
- Fake bank notifications
- Delivery scams
- Password reset scams
- Business Email Compromise (BEC)

### 7. Best Practices for Prevention
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Verify requests before responding
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Keep software updated
- Report suspicious emails

### 8. Interactive Quiz
Test your knowledge with questions covering:
- Phishing indicators
- Website verification
- Security best practices
- Social engineering awareness

## Technologies Used

- Microsoft PowerPoint / Google Slides
- Cybersecurity Awareness Content
- Interactive Quiz Components

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this training, participants will be able to:

- Identify common phishing attempts.
- Recognize fake websites and malicious emails.
- Understand social engineering techniques.
- Follow cybersecurity best practices.
- Reduce the risk of becoming a phishing victim.
